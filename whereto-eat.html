Where to eat

Culinary landscape is as vast and diverse as its geography. Whether you're into savory classics, sweet treats, or international flavors, there's something to satisfy every palate across this expansive country. Here's a glimpse into some delicious options for where to eat in Canada:

Poutine in Quebec

Start your culinary journey with Canada's iconic dish, poutine. Head to Quebec to savor this comfort food made with crispy fries, cheese curds, and smothered in savory gravy. La Banquise in Montreal is a renowned spot to try various poutine variations.

Casino Rama Resort - Orillia, Ontario

Casino Rama, situated in Orillia, features a large casino floor, entertainment venues, and a hotel with 289 rooms. The resort hosts concerts, comedy shows, and other events in addition to its gaming facilities.

View More

Caesars Windsor Hotel & Casino - Windsor, Ontario

This casino is located on the riverfront in Windsor, Ontario, and is directly across from Detroit, Michigan. It offers a wide range of gaming options, restaurants, entertainment venues, and a hotel with over 700 rooms.

View More

River Cree Resort and Casino - Edmonton, Alberta

River Cree Resort and Casino provides a comprehensive entertainment experience with a large casino, various dining options, a sports bar, and a hotel featuring 249 rooms. It is situated in Edmonton, Alberta.

View More

Grey Eagle Resort and Casino - Calgary, Alberta

Grey Eagle Resort and Casino in Calgary offers a vibrant gaming floor, multiple dining choices, and a hotel with 178 rooms. The resort also includes event spaces, a spa, and a fitness center.
